AGGASS ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED
COMPANY PROFILE

AGGASS ENGINEERING COMPANY Limited was incorporated in December 2005 under the Ghana Company Code as a limited liability company. The head office is in Ntotroso on Ntotroso Kenyasi Road near the NADeF Secretariat. The company aims to become one of the leading companies in civil engineering construction and mining.

Vission

Our vision is to be the standard for providing excellent and quality construction and engineering services in the construction and mining industries. As such, we are motivated to consistently offer our services to our clients and the communities in which we work with safety and integrity.

Mission

To develop long-lasting client relationships by providing exceptional stakeholders value in an environment of trust and respect.

BUSINESS STRATEGY

  • Aggass Eng Co Ltd is committed to ensuring that the products/materials used on its projects are safe and approved by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA). Along with its clients, the company believes in a health and safety initiative that is all pervasive, managing any potential loss in the work environment.
  • The Company has adopted a corporate strategy that is dedicated to improving the performance of activities on the critical path of its clients' projects. The company does this by building on its core strengths, innovative equipment, and design engineering expertise, project and site management, within an environment of safety excellence.

OUR CORE VALUES

  1. Integrity - Our business conduct includes the highest standards of honesty, ethics and virtue. We strive not to endanger employees, clients or our company
  2. Quality – We ensure that projects are finished within the guidelines established by the Scope of Work
  3. Reliability – Customers can also rely on us as we always deliver our services on time
  4. Teamwork – We strive to achieve our goals by combining our skills and knowledge and sharing our responsibilities as a team.
  5. Safety and environment - Our safety and that of our employees are very important to us. We also ensure that our activities do not harm the environment and take measures to ensure a sustainable environment.

SAFTEY

All employees and partners receive regular safety training to ensure continued safety in all our endeavors. This is to keep everyone informed of new and improved safety protocols and to build confidence in safety.

THE C.E.O

Mr Aggass has been known for his civil works and project completion in Ahafo Region. He is 25 years experienced in civil engineer who has manage this company till date and has completed most awarded project form Newmont Gold mining company in Ghana
