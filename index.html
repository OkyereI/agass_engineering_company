AGGASS ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED COMPANY PROFILE

AGGASS ENGINEERING COMPANY Limited was incorporated in December 2005 under the Ghana Company Code as a limited liability company. The head office is in Ntotroso on Ntotroso Kenyasi Road near the NADeF Secretariat. The company aims to become one of the leadingÂ companies in civil engineering construction and mining.

Vission

Our vision is to be the standard for providing excellent and quality construction and engineering services in the construction and mining industries. As such, we are motivated to consistently offer our services to our clients and the communities in which we work with safety and integrity.

Mission

To develop long-lasting client relationships by providing exceptional stakeholders value in an environment of trust and respect.

BUSINESS STRATEGY

  • ggass Eng Co Ltd is committed to ensuring that the products/materials used on its projects are safe and approved by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA). Along with its clients, the company believes in a health and safety initiative that is all pervasive, managing any potential loss in the work environment.
  • The Company has adopted a corporate strategy that is dedicated to improving the performance of activities on the critical path of its clients' projects. The company does this by building on its core strengths, innovative equipment, and design engineering expertise, project and site management, within an environment of safety excellence.
